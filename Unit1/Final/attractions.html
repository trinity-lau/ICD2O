Famous Tourist Attractions in Italy

Explore Italy's most iconic landmarks and cultures.

Tower of Pisa

The Leaning Tower of Pisa is a popular tourist spot in Italy, famous for its tilt caused by an unstable foundation construction in the 12th century.

Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome is an ancient Roman amphitheater located in the center of the city, famous for its history and architecture.

Trevi Fountain

The Trevi Fountain is known for its sculptural beauty and tradition of tossing coins to ensure a return to Rome.

Pantheon

The Pantheon is a Roman building famous for its massive concrete dome. Originally built as a temple for all gods, later turned into a church.

Saint Mark's Basilica

Located in Venice, this architectural masterpiece combines Byzantine and Gothic styles and contains 40,000 square feet of gold mosaics.

Duomo di Milano

The largest church in Italy, built over six centuries. Its exterior features spires and gargoyles, with a golden statue of the Madonnina inside.
