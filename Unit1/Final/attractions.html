Tower of Pisa

The Leaning Tower of Pisa is a popular tourist spot in italy, its famous because of the tilt caused by a unstable foundation construction in the 12th century.

Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome is an ancient Roman amphitheater and its located in the center of Rome, Italy.

Trevi Fountain

The Trevi Fountain is often known for its sculptural structure and tourists often toss coins into the fountian to ensure their return to Rome. Its a a tradition popularized by the 1954 film Three Coins in the Fountain.

Pantheon

The Pantheon is a Roman building known for its massive concrete dome. It was originally built as a temple for all gods and later turned into a church.

Saint Mark's Basilica

It is loacted in Venice and an architectural masterpiece of Byzantine and Gothic styles. Its also contains 40,000 square feet of shimmering gold mosaics depicting biblical scenes.

Duomo di Milano

It is largest church in Italy and took nearly six centuries to build.Its exterior is adorned with numerous spires and gargoyles, while the interior contains vast stained-glass windows and a golden statue of the Madonnina at its highest point.
